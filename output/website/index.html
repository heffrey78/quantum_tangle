About the Author

Dr. Samantha Quark, the brilliant mind behind "Quantum Tangles," is a renowned physicist and bestselling science fiction author. With a Ph.D. in Quantum Mechanics from MIT and years of experience in cutting-edge research, Dr. Quark brings a unique blend of scientific accuracy and creative storytelling to her work.

The inspiration for "Quantum Tangles" came from Dr. Quark's own experiences as a young scientist and her fascination with the mysterious world of quantum mechanics. She wanted to create a story that not only entertains but also educates readers about the mind-bending concepts of quantum physics while exploring the universal themes of self-discovery, friendship, and the search for one's place in the universe.

Through the character of Zoe Chen, Dr. Quark aims to inspire young readers, especially those on the autism spectrum, to embrace their unique perspectives and pursue their passions in science and beyond. "Quantum Tangles" is a testament to the power of curiosity, perseverance, and the incredible potential of the human mind to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos.
